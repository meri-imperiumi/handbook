## Facts and figures

_Lille Ø_ is an [Amigo 40](https://sailboatdata.com/sailboat/amigo-40), a 31ft heavy displacement full-keel “spitzgatter” (canoe stern with transom-hung rudder) sailboat built in Sweden in 1979.

- Length: 9.36m
- Beam: 3.05m
- Draft: 1.55m
- Air draft: 13.37m
- Displacement: 5500kg
- Ballast: 2600kg
- Hull speed: 6.9kt
